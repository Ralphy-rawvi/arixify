FAQ

What happens after 1 year?
We'll email you 45 days before your year ends. You can continue at $4/month or $39/year (50% off, locked in forever), or switch back to the free plan. No auto-charges, no surprises. If you do nothing, you move to free after a 7-day grace period.
Do I need a credit card to apply?
No. The application is a short form. No payment info needed until you decide to continue after your free year.
How long until I get Pro unlocked?
After you submit the form, we'll confirm within 24 hours. Once you complete the review and your extra step, Pro gets activated within 48 hours. During busy weeks, it might take up to 72 hours.
Can I cancel anytime?
There's nothing to cancel. You're not subscribed to anything. After your free year, you choose whether to subscribe or not. If you subscribe later and want to stop, you can cancel through Paddle (our payment provider) at any time.
